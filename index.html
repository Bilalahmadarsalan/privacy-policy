Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026
Pashto Quran By ASDC Offline-first • Audio & Tafsir

This Privacy Policy applies to the Pashto Quran developed by ASDC ("we", "our", or "us"). This app is designed to provide Quran reading, Tafsir, Translation in multiple languages, Bookmarks, and related features, primarily working offline with optional online functionality.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email, or location.

However, the app may collect limited non-personal information automatically, including:

  • Device type and Android version
  • App usage data (for performance improvements)
  • Error logs (for debugging crashes)
No personal tracking — your privacy stays protected

2. Permissions We Use

Our app requires the following permissions to deliver a seamless Quranic experience:

  • INTERNET Used to: Download Audio Recitations content & load online resources (if available)
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE Used to: Check whether the device is connected to the internet before downloading content
  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE Used to: Read downloaded tafsir files or user-saved data from your device
  • WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE Used to: Save downloaded Audio files and other content for offline use
  • WAKE_LOCK Used to: Prevent the device from sleeping during Quran Reading

All permissions are used solely for functionality, and no data is transmitted to external servers beyond your control.

3. How We Use Information

We use the limited collected data to:

  • Improve app performance and stability
  • Fix bugs and crashes (via anonymous error logs)
  • Ensure proper functioning of downloads and offline features

No analytics are used to build user profiles or for marketing purposes.

4. Data Storage

All downloaded content (such as Audio files, Quran database, tafsir) is stored locally on your device.

We do not upload, share, or remotely store your personal data to any server. Your bookmarks, recitation preferences, and saved files stay on your phone.

5. Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services such as:

  • Google Play Services – for stability, crash reporting (Firebase Crashlytics) and, if enabled, anonymous analytics to understand general usage trends.

These services may collect limited technical data according to their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review Google's Privacy Policy for more details. No personal identifiers are shared.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, including using secure connections for downloads and minimizing permission scope. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We continuously strive to protect your privacy.

7. Children's Privacy

This app is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. Since no personal information is collected, parents can confidently allow children to use the app. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, contact us and we will promptly address it.

8. Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Delete downloaded audio files, tafsir, or any stored content anytime directly from your device storage or via app settings.
  • Uninstall the app to remove all stored data, bookmarks and preferences completely from your device.
  • Revoke permissions any time from Android settings — core reading features remain available.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in practices or for legal reasons. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the “Last updated” date will be revised. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this privacy policy or the app's data practices, please reach out — we’d love to hear from you.
